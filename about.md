## C# Investeringsbank AB

# About the project
To develop our skills in building more complex programs, we carried out a group project where we created a banking app in C#. In the project, I was responsible for designing the structure of the user classes and developing the login functionality.

I also implemented the TransactionManager class, which ensured that transfers between accounts could only be carried out every 15 minutes. This required me to carefully plan both the logic and structure to create a stable and clear solution.

The project gave me valuable experience in integrating multiple components into a working system, collaborating effectively in a team, and taking responsibility for a central part of the application. At the same time, it challenged me to step outside my comfort zone and grow technically.
